# IT Business & Ethics

## IT Ethics
- **Privacy**: Data minimization, consent, anonymization
- **Intellectual Property**: Copyright (code), patents (inventions), trademarks (brands)
- **Honesty**: Truthful advertising, no false claims
- **Professionalism**: ACM/IEEE codes, continuing education
- **Social Responsibility**: Accessibility, environmental impact (e-waste)
- **Fairness**: Avoid bias in algorithms, equitable access

## Laws and Regulations
- **GDPR**: EU, data subject rights, fines up to 4% revenue
- **CCPA**: US, right to know/delete data, opt-out
- **HIPAA**: US, health data privacy, breach notification
- **SOX**: US, financial data integrity, internal controls
- **COPPA**: US, children's online privacy, parental consent
- **DMCA**: Digital Millennium Copyright Act, takedowns
- **CFAA**: Computer Fraud and Abuse Act, unauthorized access

## Business Aspects
- **IT Governance**: COBIT, ITIL frameworks
- **Risk Management**: SWOT, risk registers
- **Compliance**: Audits, certifications (ISO 27001)
- **Vendor Management**: SLAs, contracts, due diligence
- **Cost-Benefit Analysis**: ROI, NPV, payback period
- **IT Strategy**: Porter's Five Forces, SWOT for tech

## Ethical Dilemmas
- Whistleblowing: Edward Snowden (NSA surveillance)
- Data Mining: Cambridge Analytica (Facebook data misuse)
- AI Bias: Facial recognition errors in minorities
- Cybersecurity vs. Privacy: Backdoors in encryption
- Open Source vs. Proprietary: GPL vs. closed licenses
- Automation: Job displacement, universal basic income

## Ethical Frameworks
- **Utilitarianism**: Greatest good for greatest number
- **Deontology**: Duty-based, rules (Kant)
- **Virtue Ethics**: Character traits
- **ACM Code**: Contribute to society, avoid harm, be honest
